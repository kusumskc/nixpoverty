WHAT IS POVERTY

We can define poverty as the condition where the basic needs of a family, like food, shelter, clothing, and education are not fulfilled. It can lead to other problems like poor literacy, unemployment, malnutrition, etc. A poor person is not able to get education due to lack of money and therefore remains unemployed. An unemployed person is not able to buy enough & nutritious food for his family and their health decline

CAUSES OF POVERTY

  • Colonial Exploitation
  • Increase in population
  • Natural calamities
  • Raise of unorganised sector
  • Lack of investment
  • Social factors(illiteracy,minorities,social norms)
  • Lack of skilled labours
  • corruption
  • Inefficiant use of resources

EFFECTS OF POVERTY

Poverty affects the life of a poor family. A poor person is not able to take proper food & nutrition &his capacity to work reduces. Reduced capacity to work further reduces his income, making him poorer. Children from poor family never get proper schooling & proper nutrition. They have to work to support their family & this destroys their childhood. Some of them may also involve in crimes like theft, murder, robbery, etc. A poor person remains uneducated & is forced to live under unhygienic conditions in slums.
